BarcodeScanner Plugin Demo

The BarcodeScanner plugin supports extracting data from a large range of barcodes, including QR codes.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS/Android/Windows Phone - App package.

Scanning

Scan a QR code or any type of barcode. Front camera and flip are available on iOS and Android (provided the device has multiple cameras).

Encoding (iOS)

Encode some piece of text into a QR code.
